Best Practices for Title Tags
  • Optimal length: 30-60 characters for best display in search results
  • Unique titles: Each page should have a unique title tag
  • Include keywords: Naturally incorporate primary keywords at the beginning
  • Brand inclusion: Include your brand name when appropriate
  • Compelling copy: Write titles that encourage clicks
  • Accurate description: Accurately describe the page content
  • Avoid keyword stuffing: Keep titles natural and readable
