Privacy Policy

Last updated: DATE HERE

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information within our mobile application (“the App”). We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. This includes:

  • No name
  • No email
  • No photos
  • No account information
  • No user-entered data

No personal or sensitive information is requested, stored, or shared.

2. Information Automatically Collected

The App uses trusted third-party services that may automatically collect non-personal information for analytics, advertising, and crash reporting. This may include:

  • Device details
  • App performance and usage data
  • Advertising identifiers

The third-party services we use are:

  • Google AdMob (Ads)
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google Play Services

For more information, please review their privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Play Services: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Permissions We Request

The App may request the following permissions:

Location Permission

Used only for features that require location access. We do not store or share your location.

Notifications Permission

Used to send app-related reminders or alerts. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

4. How We Use Data

Any automatically collected data is used solely for:

  • Improving app performance
  • Diagnosing crashes
  • Enhancing the user experience
  • Displaying relevant ads

We do not sell, trade, or share data except through the third-party systems already listed.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Security

We rely on secure, industry-standard infrastructure provided by Google and Firebase to protect any data processed by their services.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Updates will be posted within the App or on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
