1. Information We Collect

Personal Data

We may collect personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, and profile image when you register for an account or update your profile. This information is used to personalize your experience, synchronize your data across devices, and facilitate household collaboration features.

Device Permissions

We may request access to certain features from your mobile device, including:

  • Camera: Used strictly for scanning QR codes to link devices and join households. We do not record or save video/images from your camera.
  • Notifications: Used to send you reminders for cleaning routines and household updates.

You can change our access or permissions at any time in your device's settings.

App Activity Data

We collect data regarding your usage of the App, such as completed cleaning tasks, routine schedules, and points earned. This data is stored to track your progress and provide the core functionality of the App.

2. Use of Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the App to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Synchronize your cleaning routines and progress across multiple devices.
  • Enable household collaboration features (e.g., sharing status with family members).
  • Send you reminders and notifications regarding your schedules.
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience with the App.

Google Firebase
